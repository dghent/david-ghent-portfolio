Basic Blading Tricks

A guide to most of the basic tricks you should try to learn as a new blader. A little side project with all the time I had sheltering in place during the pandemic of 2020.

#rollerbladingisback

Frontside

Probably the best beginner trick to start with. Approach with speed and jump with both feet and land equally balanced on the center of your frames, with your legs shoulder width apart. Angle your ankles outward to balance.

Backside

Similar to a frontside, but slightly more difficult with the rail to your back. You'll find it'll feel like you have to turn a bit more than a frontside.

Royale

Much like a frontside, but lean both your ankles backward. You'll find it's much easier to bend with your knees to allow your ankles to rest. Make sure you lean forward otherwise you'll slip backward.
